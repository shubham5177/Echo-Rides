Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card in the renter's name, and a passport or ID card. For international renters, an International Driving Permit might be required depending on your country of origin.

Is there a minimum age requirement?

Yes, the standard minimum age is 21 years. Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge. Some specialty and luxury vehicles may have a higher minimum age requirement of 25 or 30 years.

How does the electric vehicle charging work?

Our electric vehicles come with a charging cable and a map of all charging stations along popular routes. Many of our partner locations offer free charging. The vehicles typically have a range of 250-300 miles per charge.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the scheduled pickup time receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24-48 hours receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 24 hours before pickup are non-refundable.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between our locations. Additional fees may apply depending on the distance between pickup and drop-off locations. Please specify your one-way rental needs during the booking process.

What happens if I return the car late?

We provide a 59-minute grace period after your scheduled return time. Returns beyond this period will incur additional hourly charges up to a maximum of one extra day's rental cost.
